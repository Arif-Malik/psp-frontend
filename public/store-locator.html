About this Store

This store is located on Cooley Lake Rd between Williams Lake Rd and Union Lake Rd. We Currently Share our plaza with Aco Hardware, Advanced Auto, Dollar Tree and Oreilly's Auto. This store was opened in July, 1991 by long time franchisees and friends Addy Shattuck and Uda Shallop. This store was the first Pet Supplies Plus to have "Store Cats" Permanent residents free roaming the store meeting, greeting and hitching rides with our customers. Current store cat (Ozzy).
